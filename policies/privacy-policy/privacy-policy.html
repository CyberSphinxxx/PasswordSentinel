PasswordSentinel's Privacy Policy

Effective Date

November 5, 2024

Overview

PasswordSentinel is a free, locally-run, open-source tool designed for personal password generation and management. All data is processed and stored locally on your device, and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Data Collection

PasswordSentinel does not collect or store any personal data:

  • All passwords and data are stored locally on your device only
  • No data is transmitted or saved on external servers
  • No personal information is collected or stored

Data Usage

Since all data is stored locally on your device, PasswordSentinel does not have access to, or control over, your stored data. This application is designed to function independently of any server-based data collection or processing.

User Responsibility

  • Ensuring your device is secure and protected with a strong password
  • Regularly updating your device's security settings and applying software updates
  • Keeping your local storage data secure

Third-Party Services

PasswordSentinel does not use any third-party services, trackers, or analytics. You can use PasswordSentinel with confidence that no personal information or usage data is shared with external entities.

Children's Privacy

PasswordSentinel is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store any information from children under this age, as no data collection is conducted by the application.
